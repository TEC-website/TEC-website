Who are We?

About Us

This Excellent Church is an evangelical congregation with a heartfelt mission: to reshape values and reconcile men back to God. Located in Lagos, Nigeria, our vision is to see multitudes across our nation come to know Jesus, delight in Him, and find ultimate satisfaction in Him. As a Reformed church, we hold firmly to the Three Forms of Unity and embrace the Continuationist perspective, affirming the enduring work of the Holy Spirit in empowering saints for service within God's household. We are formally registered as Phos Anthropos Global Ministry under the Nigerian Corporate Affairs Commission.

Our Creeds

We are a part of the Apostolic Church of Jesus Christ and thus affirm the Nicene Creed and Chalcedonian Creed which embody the orthodox Christian beliefs. We hold to the Three Forms of Unity of the Protestant Reformation consisting of The Belgic Confession, The Canons of Dort and The Heidelberg Catechism.
